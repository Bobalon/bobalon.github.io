Puffer Smash
  • Underwater king-of-the-hill fighting game

MY ROLE

  • Responsible for Concept Art
  • Responsible for 2D Mockups


“Puffer Smash” is a King-of-the-Hill style Fighting game, inspired by the likes of Super Smash Bros and Fly Punch Boom. The goal is to push all rivals out of the aquarium and be the only fish left inside. A one-week design exercise, before we started building games in earnest.



I sketched out some puffer fish from photographic reference, trying to get a sense of the characters and how much they’d change when puffed up. I posted regular updates in our discord channel for feedback. Over time, the design moved in a more cartoony direction, to match the tone of the game.






    Troll-themed Phone Menu
  • Made in Figma

MY ROLE

  • Art and Design
  • Handwrote the Logo and Settings text

“Troll” is a smartphone main menu mockup inspired by nordic trolls. To evoke the creature without being too on the nose, I leaned heavily on legendary illustrator John Bauer for colours and composition. The settings menu uses tails as volume sliders.

It won IntoGames's Halloween Design Workout 2022, Creepy Creatures.
